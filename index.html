What is Qubed?

qubed.cc

Qubed is a platform made for students by student(s). With the goal of helping students all over the world achieve better academic success, Qubed aims to do just that, empowering every student to learn easier.

What is our vision?

Our vision is to live in a world where students can find it easier and more fun to learn with Qubed, an all in one student productivity tool.

When are we releasing?

With the hopes the Qubed will continue to excel, we hope to release by the end of 2024 with the first version, then later on more updates to come.
